key factors for SEO
1)CLS(Cumulative Layout Shift): Amount of shifting done when page is loaded
2)LCP(Largest Contextual paint): here we see the time taken in loading largest element of the page.
Good LCP Score is 2.5 seconds.
3)FID(First Input Delay):Time taken to process any input like clicking a button. 
Good FID Score is 100 milliseconds
